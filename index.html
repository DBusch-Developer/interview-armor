How it works

Three steps. One stronger interview answer.

1

Draw a question

Choose your level and topic — HTML, CSS, JavaScript, DOM, Git, or behavioral.

2

Speak your answer

Record your response in the browser and play it back to hear yourself clearly.

3

Review the dojo notes

Transcribe your answer, send it for AI coaching, and save the feedback.

Intermediate

Trade-offs and judgment

Advanced
